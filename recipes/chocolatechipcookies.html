Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A recipe for chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 C Butter
  • 1 C Sugar
  • 1 C Brown Sugar
  • 2 Lg Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • .25 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 C Flour
  • 2 C Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Set oven to 375F.
  2. Cream butter and sugars together.
  3. Add in eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  4. Sift together dry ingredients, then gently combine with the butter mixture.
  5. Add in chocolate chips when flour has slightly incorporated.
  6. Divide into 24 equal balls and bake for 12 minutes.
