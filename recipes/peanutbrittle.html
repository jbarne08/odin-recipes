Peanut Brittle Recipe

Description

Recipe for peanut brittle, a type of candy. It makes a great gift!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of dry roasted peanuts
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup corn syrup
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Roast your peanuts.
  2. Mix all candy ingredients, including sugar, syrup and water.
  3. Cook on medium heat until is reaches 320-340F.
  4. Add salt and soda, mixing while it expands.
  5. Add vanilla and peanuts.
  6. Spread onto greased 9x13 pan. Let cool for an hour before breaking into chunks.
