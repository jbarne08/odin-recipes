Homemade Marshmallow

Description

This is a challenging recipe to make marshmallow at home.

Ingredients

  • 14.5 OZ Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Corn Syrup
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 7 tsp gelatine, bloomed in 1/2 cup of water
  • Pinch Salt
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 Cup Confectioners Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Corn Starch

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, syrup, and water in a pot while the gelatine blooms.
  2. Heat on medium high gently swirling until the mixture then place a lid on the pot for a couple minutes
  3. Using a candy thermometer, cook the mixture until 242-245F
  4. Prepare your 8x8 pan while the candy is heating, by coating with butter and applying an ample mix of powdered sugar and corn starch.
  5. In your stand mixer, add in the gelatine, and slowly drizzle in the heated candy while the mixer is going.
  6. Whisk mixture 5-6 minutes, until fluffy, then add vanilla, mix.
  7. Pour mix into prepared pan, dust with more sugar/starch mix. Let cool at room temp. for one day.
